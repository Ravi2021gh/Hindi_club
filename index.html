The Hindi Club at VIT Bhopal invites you to become a member of its community. Hindi Club VIT is one of the largest cultural communities in VIT University aiming to promote our culture. It is a cultural club dedicated to showcase the cultural heritage of Hindi speaking regions of our nation. It is a common platform for intercollege regional activities.

About Us

We aim on promoting the Hindi language, literature, and culture among the college students. The club can organize various activities such as Hindi poetry recitation, story writing, debates, and quizzes, and cultural events such as dance performances and traditional festivals celebrations. The primary goal of the Hindi club is to create awareness and interest among students about the rich heritage of Hindi language and culture and provide a platform to showcase their talent and skills. Joining a Hindi club can help students to, develop a deeper understanding of Hindi literature, and connect with like-minded people who share the same interest in the language and culture.

Benefits of joining our club

  • Our club ensures to enrich your experience, create a diverse community, encourage involvement, and provide opportunities for students.
  • This helps you to know more about our traditional heritage and generate deep understanding of our culture.
  • Open to all students, we organize programs, events, and meetings throughout the academic year.

The following will be the procedure for selection:

  • Interested participants must fill out the form to be eligible for selection.

  • Members from the core committee will shortlist the participants for the interview.

  • These shortlisted participants will be reviewed by the remaining members and the faculty advisor.

  • If the participant’s application is shortlisted, we would be assigning a task, based on the field the participant has applied for and for participating in task round you would be asked to pay a certain amount.

  • If the participant qualifies in the task round, the final interview will be conducted with the club President, Technical and Non-technical Leads.

  • Once you receive your interview selection results then voila! You would now be a part of our Club.
